Pro Streaming & Betting Tips

  1. Use two screens - Stream on one, bet on another
  2. Watch for patterns - Teams pressing, tired players, tactical changes
  3. Note the delay - Streams are 5-10 seconds behind live odds
  4. Focus on one match - Quality over quantity for better decisions
  5. Check stream quality - Lower quality = less delay
